Outreach

Planetaria

Warwick Planetarium

During my time at the University of Warwick, I was a member of the team that ran the university's planetarium. I helped set up and take down the dome and delivered custom shows using Stellarium at numerous school visits and festivals

Public Talks

I have given numerous public talks to amateur societies such as the Irish Astronimical Association (IAA) and at public events like Astronomy on Tap Belfast

Events and festivals

I have been involved in the planning and delivery of public events and festivals. I have run a stall at Astronomy day during the Northern Ireland Science Festival and voluteered at the Big Bang Festival in Birmingham.
